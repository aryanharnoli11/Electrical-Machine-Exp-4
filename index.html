Note: Click on the “Auto Connect” button to make all the connections automatically.


STEP 2: Click on "Check"button to check the connections.


STEP 3: If connections are correct, turn ON the DC supply


STEP 4: Now, move the starter handle from left to right


STEP 5: First, set the field resistance by moving the field rheostat knob.


STEP 6: Now, click on the “Add to Table” button to add readings to the observation table


STEP 7: Now vary the armature resistance by moving the armature rheostat knob to take different readings.


STEP 8:Repeat steps 6 to 7 to add more readings in the observation table.


STEP 9: Click on the "Graph" button to generate a Graph.


STEP 10: Click on the “Report” button to generate a summarised report of the simulation.


STEP 11: Click on the “Print” button to print the simulation.


STEP 12: Click on the“Reset” button to reset the simulation.
