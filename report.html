Summary

Aim

To study the speed control of a DC shunt motor by armature resistance control method, and plot the graph between the speed and armature voltage by varying the armature resistance.

Simulation Summary

The connections were completed as per the instructions. The DC supply was switched ON, the starter handle was moved from left to right to start the motor safely, and the field resistance was set. The armature resistance was then varied step by step, and the corresponding armature voltage and motor speed readings were recorded in the observation table. A Speed vs. Armature Voltage graph was plotted using the recorded data.

Components and Key Parameters

  • DC Supply
  • 3-Point Starter: 220V DC, 7.5 HP
  • DC Shunt Motor: 5 HP, 220 V DC, 19 A (max), 1500 RPM
  • Field Rheostat: 300 ohm, 3A
  • Armature Rheostat: 75 ohm, 5A
  • DC Voltmeter: 0-420 V
  • DC Ammeter: 0-1 A
  • RPM Indicator: 0-2000 RPM
  • Connecting Leads
